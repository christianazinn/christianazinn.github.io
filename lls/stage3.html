Stage 3 ~ Darkness

BGM: Bad Apple!!

Stage 3 is the first big resource dump stage. You'll be suiciding several times and using a good amount of score bombs here, and it features one of the coolest grazes in the whole game (that you will probably need to practice quite a bit). Expect to hit the graze cap here for the first time. It's a very important stage for scoring, so make sure to take it seriously!
