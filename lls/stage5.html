Stage 5 ~ Phantasmagoria

BGM: Lotus Love

Short stage, short boss. There isn't even a midboss here! There's one score bomb used at the start of the stage, and then one of the most difficult patterns in the entirety of the series that will most definitely be a survival bomb for the vast majority of people. The boss has some easy grazes (well, they're not trivial on lower difficulties, but easy on Lunatic), and getting high graze and a high cancel is consistent.
