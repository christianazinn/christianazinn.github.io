Stage 4 ~ Dream of Frail Girl

BGM: Alice Maestra

Stage 4 is the next big resource dump, and you get a lot of points here. Between three overall cancels, a plethora of score bombs, and some particularly difficult patterns, you can expect to use a lot of bombs to the point you barely need to know how to dodge the base patterns. You have a lot of opportunities to graze, so you should always hit the graze cap. The stage boss also changes based on character (each protagonist fights the other), so the route changes for each character, and here Reimu has an additional optional milk on the Marisa boss.
