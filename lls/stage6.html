Stage 6 ~ Raspberry Trap

BGM: Dream Land

Final stage, fairly anticlimactic final boss. You'll want to clear with 0 resources (lives/bombs) remaining, so all of them will be used here. The stage features some score bombs that you can cut on the fly if you aren't confident with your resources, and a free extend, bomb, and full powerup at the end of the stage. The boss herself isn't too bad, with a fairly difficult graze, a massive resource dump, and an annoying final pattern.
