Introduction to LLS Scoring

You're here! Great! That probably means you're interested in scoring LLS. Regardless of how much you know about the game itself, this guide will hopefully walk you through the process of learning how to do exactly that.

First, some things that need to be said. This guide is applicable for any shot on Normal, Hard, and Lunatic, although specific resource routes may differ between shots and difficulties. I personally am most familiar with the Reimu shots, particularly the Lunatic ReimuB category, so coverage for other shots may be spotty. The resource route is not applicable to Easy due to it not having Stage 6, and Extra is not covered here because I don't know much about its scoring. However, the mechanics are the same. Keep in mind, however, this entire guide is written with Lunatic in mind, even though it applies to other difficulties as well.

This guide is also not an entire-game guide, it assumes you know a very small baseline of mechanics and jargon. If you don't know what graze or dream is, that's fine (just check out the jargon dictionary) but if you don't know how to focus or bomb, that's going to be an issue. If you aren't confident you know enough, do check out the wiki gameplay page for the basics (although don't trust it for anything further! The wiki is usually very inaccurate).

This site also contains more advanced information that I wanted to collate in one place, so it isn't just scattered all over the Internet. If you're just learning, you can skip most of these, but I think they're usually nice to know!

Think you know what you're doing? Great. Let's get started.

How to Use This Guide

This guide is segmented into several sections, covering the important topics of scoring Lotus Land Story. Feel free to take the time to brush up on scoring mechanics if you don't know them yet, since understanding the system is crucial to understanding what's going on in the rest of the guide. The guide will also use a variety of terms that you might not understand; these are mostly defined in the jargon dictionary, and if they're not, tell me to add it! There's also a quickstart guide for players new to LLS scoring to peruse.

Most importantly, this guide includes in-depth analysis and guidance for several different routes and optional alterations, so you can tailor your route for your own skill level and score goal. Some strategies are only used in certain routes and some routes do certain sections differently, and these are marked as such when they show up, like so:

⛤ Safe route only:

Such specifications, as well as difficulty names (Easy, Normal, Hard, Lunatic, and the occasional Extra), shottype/character names (Reimu, Marisa), point/power item names, and bomb, suicide, extend, and graze locations, will be color-coded to make it easier to find relevant information.

Each page contains a whole lot of information about its relevant topic, and they are written under the assumption that you are reading them in order. If you only want information about a certain section, though, feel free to skip ahead! Each page contains a number of clips of sections, and some text explaining it as well, so you can choose what to use depending on what learning style you enjoy most.

Each section will also have a tl;dr section at the end for ease of use - I know it gets wall-of-text-y at times.
