Stage 1 ~ Phantom Land/Phantom Night

BGM: Witching Dream/Selene's light

Note: The stage name, BGM, and background change depending on character. However, the stage itself is the same.

As the first stage, you can expect it to be fairly easy. There are some rudimentary score bombs and grazes in the stage, but the stage only gives around 15m max. As you get more experience, these routes will become ingrained in your muscle memory.

  • Stage Section 1
  • Midboss: Bewitching Lotus Flower
  • Stage Section 2
  • Boss: Orange

Stage Section 1

Position yourself on the left, and go up to collect all the items after killing the rows of bakebake. Move over to the right afterwards and do the same with the next rows of bakebake.

Midboss: Bewitching Lotus Flower
