Jargon Dictionary

  • LLS: abbreviation for Lotus Land Story
  • graze: when you get near a bullet but don't get hit by it
  • supergraze: any strat that gives a lot of graze from a singular attack
  • strat: shorthand for strategy
  • shot/shottype: the subtype and character combination used, which affects the bullets you shoot
  • pattern: a specific attack used by a midboss or boss, can also refer to stage sections
